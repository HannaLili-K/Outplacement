# Outplacement
**Outplacement with a Human Touch:**
Restructuring is a reality for every growing company. Personally, I believe there’s nothing wrong with people moving on, as long as it’s handled with genuine care and clear intention.

In my experience, many large companies are unaware of the amazing talent they already possess. Often, it’s actually cheaper to retrain someone than to let them go. I enjoy speaking with these professionals to determine if there’s a way for them to remain within the company in a new role.

But when a departure is inevitable, how you say goodbye matters.

**How can I help your team?**
While your HR and legal departments handle the logistics, I step in as the supportive partner who focuses on the person behind the title.
- **Career Strategy:** We sit down and talk through the "what’s next." I listen, and we figure out the best strategy for their next move.
- **LinkedIn & CV Makeover:** I help them transform their profiles and resumes so they feel confident stepping back into the market.
- **Interview Prep:** I share my experience as a recruiter to show them how to stand out.

Everyone needs someone to listen during a transition. I provide the psychological and professional safety net that ensures employees leave feeling cared for, rather than just 'sent away.

I’ve done this for large corporations before, and what I’ve seen is that people just want to be heard. It’s a great feeling for them to have someone to talk to during a stressful time, and honestly, I love being that person for them.

**The Goal:** To make sure your employees leave with a good story to tell about your company, feeling supported, not just "sent away."
Are you planning a transition or restructuring? Let’s work together to make sure your people are supported every step of the way.
